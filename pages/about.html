About

Plain-O Brewing started with a simple question: why does beer need a complicated name?

We stripped it back. No wordplay, no seasonal mythology, no limited-release hype. Just beer. Named for what it looks like in the glass.

Blue. Green. Red. Yellow. Each one brewed to be exactly what it sounds like — honest, well-made, and worth coming back to.

We're not trying to reinvent craft beer. We're trying to make it a little less exhausting to order.

Pull up a stool. Tell us what color you're in the mood for.

Plain-O Brewing Co. Drink simple.
